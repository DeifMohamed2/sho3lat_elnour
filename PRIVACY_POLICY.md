# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 2024**

## Introduction

Welcome to Sho3lat Elnour Schools ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you provide directly to us when you use our App, including:

- **Account Information**: Phone number, password, and authentication credentials
- **Student Information**: Full name, student ID, date of birth, grade, class, enrollment date, and academic records
- **Parent/Guardian Information**: Full name, phone number, email address, and physical address
- **Contact Information**: Phone numbers, email addresses, and mailing addresses

### 1.2 Educational Information

We collect educational and academic information related to students, including:

- **Attendance Records**: Daily attendance status, entry and exit times, and attendance history
- **Academic Performance**: Grades, test scores, certificates, and academic achievements
- **Class Information**: Class schedules, timetables, and course information
- **School Communications**: Messages, announcements, and notifications from the school and teachers

### 1.3 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our App, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage patterns, including:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information
- **Usage Data**: App usage patterns, features accessed, time spent in the App, and interaction data
- **Technical Information**: IP address, browser type, and other technical identifiers

### 1.4 Location Information

We do not currently collect precise location information. However, if location services are enabled in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Service Provision

- To provide, maintain, and improve our school management services
- To authenticate users and manage accounts
- To display student profiles, attendance records, grades, and academic information
- To facilitate communication between parents, students, and school administrators
- To send notifications, messages, and important announcements

### 2.2 Educational Services

- To track and manage student attendance
- To maintain academic records and grades
- To provide access to timetables and class schedules
- To manage certificates and academic achievements
- To facilitate parent-teacher communication

### 2.3 Account Management

- To create and manage user accounts
- To verify user identity and prevent unauthorized access
- To process login requests and maintain account security
- To manage multiple student profiles under a single parent account

### 2.4 Communication

- To send important notifications about school events, attendance, and academic updates
- To deliver messages from teachers and school administrators
- To respond to user inquiries and provide customer support

### 2.5 Legal and Safety

- To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
- To protect the rights, property, and safety of users, students, and the school
- To detect, prevent, and address fraud, security issues, and technical problems
- To enforce our terms of service and other policies

### 3.2 Service Providers

  - Security and fraud prevention services

### 3.3 Legal Requirements

- We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation
- We may share information to respond to legal processes, enforce our policies, or protect rights and safety

### 3.4 Business Transfers

- In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity, subject to the same privacy protections

### 3.5 With Your Consent

- We may share information with your explicit consent or at your direction

## 4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

- **Encryption**: We use encryption technologies to protect data in transit and at rest
- **Access Controls**: We limit access to personal information to authorized personnel only
- **Secure Authentication**: We require secure passwords and authentication mechanisms
- **Regular Security Audits**: We conduct regular security assessments and updates
- **Data Backup**: We maintain secure backups of your information

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

- Provide our services to you
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements
- Maintain academic and administrative records as required by educational regulations

When information is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it in accordance with our data retention policies and applicable laws.
